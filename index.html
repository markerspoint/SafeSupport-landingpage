OUR MISSION

Why We Created SafeSupport

At Central Philippine State University (CPSU), we noticed a growing concern for students’ mental health. Many students struggle with stress, anxiety, and feelings of isolation—issues that, if left unaddressed, can escalate into serious consequences, including thoughts of self-harm.

SafeSupport was created as a capstone project to provide a safe, accessible, and compassionate platform where students can seek counseling, find resources, and connect with professional support before their challenges become overwhelming.

Our goal is simple: help students listen, heal, and grow—so that every student feels supported, understood, and never alone.
